Our Mission

Artificial intelligence aims to learn human cognitive abilities and eventually surpass it. Currently, most AI models rely on deep neural networks, inspired by the human brain, which takes in a lot of raw data and expected results and learns the relationship between the two.


The Artiﬁcial Intelligence (AI) mission of the Hybe focuses on efforts that will beneﬁt India in addressing societal needs in areas such as healthcare, education, agriculture, smart cities, and infrastructure, including smart mobility and transportation.


Artificial neural networks are currently used for the complex analysis in various fields, ranging from medicine to engineering, and these networks can be used to design the next generation of computers.
